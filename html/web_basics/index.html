Savannah Cats

Savannah cats are a hybrid between domestic cats and the African serval

This is an image of a savannah cat next to a serval, courtesy of Pictures-of-Cats.

Savannah cats are even better than normal cats for several reasons:

  1. They are affectionate and social animals! They are often said to be "dog-like" in their loyalty and temperament.
  2. They are ninja-like athletes! From a standing position, they can easily leap 8 or more feet!
  3. You get the best of both worlds! They can use a litter box, can be harness-trained to walk on a leash, and they can learn to play fetch.
